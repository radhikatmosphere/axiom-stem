# AXIOM limitations

- AXIOM’s genetics lesson is a simple, single-gene Punnett-square model. It does not model complex inheritance, phenotype prediction, health outcomes, or personal genetic information.
- Combinatorics inputs are deliberately bounded for a responsive demo. Results are counting outputs, not probability predictions unless a probability model is explicitly supplied.
- The verifier checks an implemented subset of factual risk: citations, uncited factual segments, and practical numerical/formula/unit tokens. It does not establish complete semantic truth.
- OpenAI narration is optional and may be unavailable, slow, or produce output that fails validation. AXIOM falls back visibly.
- The current per-process rate limiter is basic; production should use platform-managed rate controls.
- Chandas/Sanskrit prosody is disabled. No claim of a scholarly scansion engine is made until expert-reviewed rules and fixtures are available.
- Benchmark latency is local-process measurement, not an end-to-end production performance guarantee.
